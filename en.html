Professor, School of Computer Science, Fudan University · Lead of OpenMOSS

Xipeng Qiu is a Professor at the School of Computer Science, Fudan University, and a CAAI Fellow. He received his B.S. and Ph.D. degrees from Fudan University. His research focuses on large language models, multimodal foundation models, and AI agents.

He leads the development of the open-source LLM MOSS and the NLP toolkits FastNLP / FudanNLP, widely adopted in academia and industry. His students have received the CAAI / CIPSC Best Dissertation Award, Microsoft Research Asia Fellowship, and Baidu / ByteDance Scholarships.
